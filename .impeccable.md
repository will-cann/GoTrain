## Design Context

### Users
Primary user is the creator — a developer/athlete who wants AI-generated weekly training plans based on Strava data. Secondary audience: other fitness-minded developers if open-sourced. Also serves as a portfolio piece demonstrating coding ability and design taste.

### Use Cases
1. Generate a personalized weekly training plan from Strava activity data + stated goals
2. Modify/discuss the plan conversationally with an AI coach chatbot
3. Review recent activity history from Strava

### Brand Personality
**Intuitive, Empowering, Grounding**

NYC street running culture meets contemporary athletic minimalism. Think run club flyers wheat-pasted on brick walls, not corporate fitness apps. The interface should feel like it belongs in the same world as Orchard Street Runners and LAAMS — raw, confident, designed with restraint but unmistakable attitude.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Dark, minimal, typographically bold — streetwear gallery meets athletic utility
- **References**: [Orchard Street Runners](https://www.orchardstreetrunners.com/) (dark athletic minimalism, video-forward, sophisticated restraint) meets [LAAMS](https://laams.nyc/) (uppercase everything, austere gallery aesthetic, dense grid, Helvetica-driven)
- **Anti-references**: Generic fitness apps (MyFitnessPal, Fitbod), anything with gradient cards, glowing accents, or rounded-everything SaaS aesthetics
- **Theme**: Dark mode
- **Typography**: Bold sans-serif, uppercase accents, tight tracking, strong hierarchy
- **Color**: Primarily monochrome (near-black backgrounds, off-white text) with two brand accent colors used sparingly — orange `#ff751f` (primary accent) and green `#00bf63` (secondary/status)

### Design Principles
1. **Street-level confidence**: Design like a run club, not a startup. Bold type, raw layouts, no filler.
2. **Typography carries the weight**: Let type do the heavy lifting — not icons, not gradients, not decorative elements.
3. **Earned minimalism**: Every element must earn its place. If it doesn't serve the athlete, cut it.
4. **Dark and grounded**: Dark backgrounds, high contrast, no glow. Feels solid, not floating.
5. **Utility with attitude**: Functional and fast, but with unmistakable design point-of-view.
